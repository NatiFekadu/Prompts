## Reverse Mortgage Key Facts
- Available to homeowners age 62 and older
- No monthly mortgage payments required
- FHA-insured HECM loans available
- Loan repaid when homeowner moves, sells, or passes away
- Homeowner retains ownership of the home
- **Ongoing responsibilities:** Property taxes, homeowners insurance, home maintenance
- Amount depends on age, home value, and current interest rates
