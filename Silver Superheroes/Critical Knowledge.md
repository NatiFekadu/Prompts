## Reverse Mortgage Key Facts
Homeowners 62+. No monthly mortgage payments. FHA-insured HECM. Loan repaid when homeowner moves, sells, or passes. Homeowner retains ownership. **Ongoing responsibilities:** property taxes, homeowners insurance, maintenance. Amount depends on age, home value, current rates.
