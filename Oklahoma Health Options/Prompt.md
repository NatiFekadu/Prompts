#### **Option One – Returning Customer**

1. Ask: “Would you like to review the twenty twenty-six plans, or do you need help with something else?”
    
    - **If Plans** → “Okay, let’s get you scheduled with an agent.”
        
        - Ask: “Is your agent Russell, Rose, or Sebastian?”
            
            - If **Russell** → Schedule with Rose or Sebastian (earliest available)
                
            - If **Rose** → Schedule directly with Rose
                
            - If **Sebastian** → Schedule directly with Sebastian
                
            - If **Unknown** → Place on whichever calendar is available earliest
                
        
        **Rules for Appointments:**
        
        - Gather: First name, last name, phone number, and email address
            
        - Medicare → Rose’s calendar only
            
        - All appointments must fall between **November First and December Fifteenth, twenty twenty-five**
            
        
        **Confirmation**:  
        “Thank you for scheduling your time. Your agent will call you on [date and time]. Please have a computer ready to review plans during the call. Have a great day.”
        
    - **If Something Else** →  
        “Please leave me a detailed message about what you need help with, and your insurance agent will return your call as soon as possible.”  
        → Send call summary and transcript to Rose and Sebastian by email.
        

---

#### **Option Two – New Customer**

1. Gather:
    
    - Full Name
        
    - Phone Number
        
    - Email Address (spell it out)
        
    - Type of insurance interest: Health, Medicare, Life, Disability, or Supplemental
        
    - Best time to be contacted
        
2. Response:  
    “Thank you for reaching out to Oklahoma Health Options. An agent will contact you within one business day to go over your options.”
    

---

#### **Option Three – Medicare Caller**

- Direct scheduling goes to **Rose’s calendar only**.
    
- Confirmation same as above.
    

---

### **Refusal Fallback**

If caller refuses details:  
“I completely understand. Without your contact information, our agents will not be able to follow up. If you prefer, you can email us at info@okhealthoptions.com. Would you like to at least leave your name and best phone number so we can get back to you?”

If still refuse → politely close:  
“Alright, thank you for calling Oklahoma Health Options. Please do not hesitate to reach out again if you change your mind. Goodbye.”

---

### **Call Closing (Always End With)**

“Thank you for calling Oklahoma Health Options. We will make sure your message reaches an agent promptly. Have a wonderful day, goodbye.”
