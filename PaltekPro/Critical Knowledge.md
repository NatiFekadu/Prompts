## What PaltekPro Does
PaltekPro is the local Sunspace Sunroom expert for the Cincinnati-Dayton region. They build porch enclosures, sunrooms, patio covers, aluminum decks, railings, bifold doors, privacy walls, and pergolas — transforming outdoor space into usable year-round living space.

## Product Lines

### Sunrooms
- **Screen Room** — aluminum frame with durable screens (black or white)
- **3-Season Sunroom (Vinyl)** — Sunspace Model 200 with WeatherMaster 4-Track Vinyl Windows, 2" foam panels, 75% ventilation
- **3-Season Sunroom (Glass)** — 2" extruded insulated aluminum pillars, single-glazed glass, aluminum-framed double roller windows
- **4-Season / Insulated Sunroom** — 3" high-density foam panels, 3" aluminum extrusions, thermal breaks, double-glazed Low-E Argon gas glass

### Patio Covers
- **Solid Insulated** — 3"/4"/6" thickness options, R-12/R-16/R-24
- **Acrylic** — 30-year yellowing warranty, high light transmission
- **Hybrid** — combines acrylic and solid, controlled sunlight plus insulation

### Decks & Railing
- **AlumaDeck** — aluminum decking, watertight, slip-resistant, fire-resistant, planks up to 22' long, 6" wide
  - Colors: Grey, Cedar, Sand, Taupe, Spruce, Grey Woodgrain
- **Aluminum Picket Railing** — 30", 36", 42" standard heights
- **Aluminum Glass Railing** — 5mm tempered safety glass with aluminum pickets
- **Topless Glass Railing** — 10mm tempered safety glass, unobstructed views
- **Integrated Screen Railing** — picket railing with BetterVue screen integration

### Windows & Doors
- **WeatherMaster Windows** — Horizontal 2-Track (50% ventilation) / 4-Track (75% ventilation), ViewFlex vinyl glazing, aluminum frame
- **WeatherMaster Plus Bi-Fold Doors** — European-designed, above-ground bottom running, slim sightlines, up to 7-panel configurations

### Other
