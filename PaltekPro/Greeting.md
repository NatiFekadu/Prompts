# PaltekPro — Platform Greeting

## Primary Greeting (PaltekPro-branded)

Thank you for calling PaltekPro... this is Ava. I just need a little information to help get you to the right place.

---

## Alternative — Generic Demo Greeting

Thank you for calling... this is Ava. I just need a little information to help get you to the right place.

---

## Shorter Variant

Hi there, thanks for calling PaltekPro... this is Ava, how can I help you today?

---

## Notes

- Configured in the **platform greeting field**, NOT in the prompt's GREETING state `<SCRIPT>`.
- Keep it short — the agent's first conversational question (from the GREETING state in Prompt.md) plays right after this.
- Use "..." for natural pauses so TTS reads it with breathing room.
- Don't end with a question here — the prompt's triage question ("How can I help you today?") is the first conversational turn.
