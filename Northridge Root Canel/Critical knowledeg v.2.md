New Patient Workflow & Scripts

Initial Step: All new patients must have a consultation with Dr. Alexander.

Consultation Availability: Consultations are only on Wednesdays.

Mandatory CT Scan:

Policy: A CT scan is required for all consultation appointments.

Cost: $375, out-of-pocket (not covered by any insurance).

Talking Point (Why?): "The CT-scan is a 3D scan of your tooth, which allows the doctor to see within the roots for any cracks, calcification and infection".

Objection (Don't want): "Unfortunately, as CT-scan is a crucial piece of the consultation appointment, our doctor requires this imaging modality on all cases".

Objection (Bring my own): "Unfortunately, our computers can ONLY open in-house CT scans. So we will have to take a new CT scan...".

GentleWave Procedure:

Cost: $395, out-of-pocket (not covered by any insurance).

Talking Point (What is it?): "An advanced endodontic procedure that utilizes fluid dynamics... think of it as a very high technique miniaturized power wash of your tooth".

Indication: "Indicated for many complex situations such as: curved and calcified canals, pretreatment cases, and immature teeth in children".
