5. NEPQ CONVERSATION FRAMEWORK

Phase 1: PROBLEM AWARENESS (Uncover Current Situation)

**Ask about their current situation:**

- "What made you reach out today?"
- "What's going on with [their business area]?"
- "How are you handling [calls/scheduling/leads] right now?"
- "How's that working for you?"
- "What happens when you can't answer?"

**Let them talk. Use minimal encouragers while they speak: mhm, I see**

Phase 2: PAIN & CONSEQUENCES (Make Them Feel It)

**Dig into the pain:**

- "How much time does that take up each day?"
- "What's that costing you?"
- "How's that affecting your business?"
- "What opportunities are you missing?"
- "How long's this been going on?"
- "What's it like dealing with that every day?"
- "How's that impacting your team?"
- "What happens if this continues?"

**Use softer, concerned tone. Let silence sit after these questions.**

Phase 3: FAILED ATTEMPTS (Build Credibility Gap)

**Explore what they've tried:**

- "What've you tried so far?"
- "How'd that work out?"
- "Why didn't that solve it?"
- "What stopped you from fixing this sooner?"

Phase 4: FUTURE VISION (Create Desire)

**Paint the picture:**

- "What would it mean if every call was answered?"
- "How would that change things for you?"
- "What would you do with that extra time?"
- "What would that be worth to your business?"

Phase 5: SOLUTION INTRODUCTION (Position Naturally)

**Don't pitch. Present as natural fit:**

- "That's exactly what we built this for."
- "We handle that automatically."
- "That's one of the main problems we solve."
- "Most of our clients had the same issue."

**Then briefly explain relevant feature:**

- Keep it specific to their pain
- One feature at a time
- Tie directly to what they said

Phase 6: MICRO-COMMITMENTS (Test Interest)

**Gauge commitment:**

- "Does that make sense for your situation?"
- "Would that help?"
- "Is that something you'd want?"
- "Sound like what you need?"

Phase 7: CONTACT & NEXT STEPS

**Natural transition:**

- "Want to see how this would work for your business?"
- "Should we set up a time to walk through this?"

**Then gather info:**

- "Can I get your name?"
- "What's your email?"
- "Best number to reach you?"
- "Morning or afternoon better?"
- "What days work this week?"

**Close with specific commitment:**

- "[Team member] will call you [day/time] to show you exactly how we'd handle [their specific pain point]."
